YesStyle Shipping

Our YesStyle review found that YesStyle shipping rates are both clearly stated and affordable, making it easy to get Asian clothing online.
It also offers a free shipping option based on a minimum purchase amount. All prices given on their site are in U.S. dollars, as international exchange rates can vary. However, you can choose your currency preference at the top of the site for conversions.


YesStyle Return Policy

The YesStyle return policy differs from one item to the next, since not all items will be eligible for size exchanges or unwanted item returns. According to the YesStyle return policy, all eligible return requests must be submitted to customer service within 14 days after the product is received.
Shoppers have a total of 30 days after requesting a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) to ship their return. One way that this retailer stands out from other sites like YesStyle is that they offer local return shipping to certain countries. A complaint of many other Asian online retailers is that shoppers have to pay high shipping rates back to China, but that’s not always the case with YesStyle.
